TrackMania A1

Each cars got a speed vector (x, y). At each round, the driver can increment change both value of x and y. He can increment by 1, or decrement by 1 or let the same value for x and y.
You have to find the best path for the car !
At each move, every driver choose their next position depending on the acceleration of its car.

Grass (symbol : '.')

When the car is on the grass, it decelerates by 1 when it's abs(x) or abs(y) is more than 1. If it's already, it can stay at 1.

Crash (symbol : 'x')

When the car crashes, it adds a penalty equals to acceleration of the car; sqrt(x*x + y*y).

Water (symbol : 'o')

If the car is on water, the car can't decelerate or accelerate.
